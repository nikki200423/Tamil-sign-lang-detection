About Tamil Sign Language

Tamil Sign Language (TSL) is a visual-gestural language used by the Deaf community in Tamil Nadu and other Tamil-speaking regions. It has its own grammar and syntax distinct from spoken Tamil.

How to Use:

  1. Click "Start Camera" to activate your webcam
  2. Position your hand in the frame making a Tamil sign
  3. For manual detection: Click "Capture Sign" then "Detect Sign"
  4. For real-time detection: Click "Toggle Real-time Detection"
  5. Add detected signs to build sentences
  6. Use "Speak" to hear the translation
